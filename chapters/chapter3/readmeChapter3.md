In Chapter 3, different models were developing attendig to the species distribution range. This folder contains the scripts used for modeling:
- Wild boar: Chapter3_modeling_framework_wildBoar.R
- Red deer: Chapter3_modeling_framework_RedDeer.R
- Roe deer: Chapter3_modeling_framework_RoeDeer.R
- Fallow deer: Chapter3_modeling_framework_FallowDeer.R
- Red fox: Chapter3_modeling_framework_RedFox.R
- Badger: Chapter3_modeling_Badger.R
- Constrained species (see Table 3.1 of the PhD Dissertation): Chapter3_modeling_constrained_StepSelection.R
- Calibration plots for all models (Figures 3.5 and 3.6 of the PhD Dissertation): Chapter3_CalibrationPlots_ALL_Merged.R
- Supplementary S3-1: Chapter3_S3-1._Data&Figure.R
